Parti.Company Label Maker

Instructions:

  1. Make sure you are using Google Chrome
  2. Design your label in Powerpoint
  3. Use Windows+Shift+S to copy your design
  4. Paste into paint
  5. Overwrite "label.png" with the new design
  6. Select the number of labels you want printed below
  7. Press print
  8. Click "More settings", select A4 paper and put margins to "none"
  9. Click print using system dialog.
